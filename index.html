Ending Child Hunger, One Story At A Time

Bountiful Children's Foundation is a non-profit organization bringing life-altering nutritional supplements to children throughout the world. Simply put, our mission is to nurture the potential of infants and toddlers to lead healthy, productive, and self-reliant lives by eliminating undernutrition. 30% of children worldwide are undernourished. Few of us can comprehend the despair and helplessness felt by millions of mothers when they lock eyes with their hungry child and know they have no food to offer them.
